Data Analytics Bootcamp

My Journey at Skills City: Data Analytics Bootcamp

Participating in the Data Analytics Bootcamp at Skills City was an intensive and rewarding experience. This comprehensive course was designed to equip participants with essential skills needed to excel in the field of Data Analysis. The program provided a solid foundation in various key concepts and practical skills required for working with data effectively in real-world scenarios.

Industry Project with KPMG

▼
During the course, I had the opportunity to work on an industry project with KPMG, which was an incredibly valuable experience. Here are some highlights of the project:

- Research and Data Accuracy: Conducted research based on the proposed research question, leading to a 20% increase in data accuracy. This involved thorough data collection and analysis to ensure high-quality results.

- Effective Communication and Team Efficiency: Communicated effectively with group members to delegate tasks, enhancing team efficiency by 30%. Our clear and concise communication ensured that everyone understood their roles and responsibilities, leading to more productive collaboration.

- Standardised Data Set: Combined individual research to create a standardised data set, reducing data inconsistency by around 50%. This standardisation also ensured the integrity of data sources, providing a reliable foundation for analysis.

- Power BI Dashboard Creation: Created a Power BI Dashboard using the data set, resulting in a 25% improvement in data visualisation and reporting. The dashboard was designed in a way that non-technical stakeholders could easily understand the key metrics, facilitating better decision-making.

- Comprehensive Documentation: Developed comprehensive documentation for the operation and maintenance of the solution, which decreased onboarding time for new users by 40%. This documentation ensured that future users could quickly and efficiently understand and utilise the solution.

- Stakeholder Reporting: Created a report explaining key findings along with relevant metrics, which improved stakeholder decision-making efficiency by 35%. The report provided clear insights and actionable recommendations based on the data analysis.

Key Takeaways

▼
The Skills City Data Analytics Bootcamp and the industry project with KPMG provided a hands-on learning experience that significantly enhanced my data analysis skills. Through this course, I learned the importance of data accuracy, effective communication, and comprehensive documentation. The project with KPMG allowed me to apply these skills in a real-world context, improving my ability to create impactful data visualisations and reports that drive better decision-making.

This journey has equipped me with the skills and confidence to tackle complex data analysis projects and contribute effectively to any team. I look forward to applying these skills in my future endeavours and continuing to grow as a data analyst.
